We are a non-profit hackerspace / workspace based out of Pomona, CA.

We are a location where people can work on projects and explore their inner makers artists and gadgeteers with a community of like minded individuals.

We have a laser cutter, 3d printers, mill, lathe, drill press, grinder, wet bench, oscilloscope, logic analyzer, electronics room, and more.

The best time to meet us is at one of our open houses on the first Saturday of each month or at our public events and classes. We also have open project nights on Fridays from 7:30 PM to 10:00 PM if you are interested in joining and want to get busy with a personal project . Lastly, our boring business meetings are on Tuesday nights that run from 7:30 PM to 8:30 PM is open to the public.
